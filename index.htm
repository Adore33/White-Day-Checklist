~ Main Building, Section 1 ~

    ~~~ After First Boss, "Tree Ghost". Leave Wood Token On Desk Until You Are Done In This Area. The Janitor Should Stay Despawned. ~~~

  • 1F - Starting Area/Foyer. Find Ghost Story, #7 Secret Of The Pond. Hidden Behind The Fountain.
  • 1F - Starting Area/Foyer. Find Misc. Item, #1 Paper Origami Crane. In Grandfather Clock Drawer.
  • 1F - Boys Bathroom. Find Misc. Item, Cigarettes. On Top Of Last Toilet.
  • 1F - Workshop. Find Doodle, #2 Pigeon. On Wall.
  • 1F - Classroom 2-6. Find Doodle, #4 Ji-Hyeon Fly. On Wall.
  • 1F - Home Economics Classroom. Find Doodle, #3 Cat. On Wall.
  • 1F - Students Records Office. Find Ghost Story, #5 Ghost Tree. In Top Portion Of Copy Machine.
  • 1F - Students Records Office. Find Misc. Item, 5g Weight. Under A Desk.
  • 1F - Main Office. Find Misc. Item, Small Red Key. In lockbox. Need Toolbelt To Open.
  • 1F - North Stairs. Find Misc. Item, Love Letter. At Very Top Of Stairs.
  • 1F - South Stairs. Find Misc. Item, Typewriter N Key. On Stairs Leading To Basement.
  • 1F - South Stairs. Find Doodle, #5 Crying Angel. On Corner Column Of Stairs.
  • 1F - Classroom 2-1. Find Misc. Item, #1 Piece Of Class Schedule (Mathematics). On Class Schedule On Wall.
  • 1F - Classroom 2-2. Find Doodle, #1 Stick Insect. On Wall.
  • 1F - Classroom 2-4. Find Misc. Item, 1g Weight. In A Cabinet.
  • 1F - Classroom 2-5. Find Misc. Item, #2 Piece Of Class Schedule (Mathematics). On Class Schedule On Wall.
  • 1F - Classroom 2-5. Find Misc. Item, Report Card (2nd In Class). In A Locker.
  • 2F - Broadcasting Room. Find Ghost Story, #4 Woman Locked In The Closet. On A Table.
  • 2F - Recording Room. Find Misc. Item, Bloody Workbook. On The Floor.
  • 2F - Recording Room. Find Doodle, #10 Butterfly. On Wall.
  • 2F - Window Near Gym Storage Room. Encounter Ghost, Girl Outside The Window. Look At Bloody Handprints. "Ghost Is Passive."
  • 2F - Gym Storage Room. Find Misc. Item, Gym Teacher Cap. Inside Big Cabinet With Medals.
  • 2F - Gym Storage Room Find Misc. Item, Typewriter M Key. Opposite Side Of The Big Cabinet.
  • 2F - Long Hallway Past Gym Storage Room. Find Doodle, #6 Rat With Crutches. On Wall.
  • 2F - Back Out Of Long Hallway Area. Find Doodle, #7 Birdcage. On Wall Of Gym Storage Room.
  • 2F - Classroom 2-7. Encounter Ghost, Kid Solving A Problem. Requires Chalk And Bloody Workbook Items. "Ghost Is Passive."
  • 2F - Classroom 2-7. Find Doodle, #9 Seong - Cat. On Wall. Is Not Clickable 1st Playthrough.
  • 2F - Classroom 2-8. Find Misc. Item, Chalk. Laying On Chalkboard Tray.
  • 2F - Classroom 2-8. Find Ghost Story, #17 Voice That Beckons Me. In A Locker.
  • 2F - Classroom 2-9. Find Doodle, #8 Lollipop And SYJM. On Wall.
  • 2F - Classroom 2-11. Encounter Ghost, Attendance Ghost. Requires Gym Teacher's Cap. Look At Book On Lecture Stand. "Ghost Can Inflict Damage."
  • 2F - Classroom 2-12. Find Misc. Item, #3 Piece Of Class Schedule (Mathematics). On Class Schedule On Wall.
  • Any Classroom Locker. Encounter Ghost, Woman In The Closet. Room Lights Off And Lighter Out. Can Spam Open/Close The Same Locker To Trigger. "Ghost Is Passive."
  • All Bathrooms Except 1F Girls Bathroom. Encounter Ghost, Face Filled With Hatred. Lay Down In Furtherest Toilet From Door And Look Between Gap And Listen For Audio To Trigger. "Ghost Is Passive."
  • 1F And 2F. Encounter Ghost/Boss, Tree Ghost. Scripted Fight. Will Need Chemical Solvent And Insect Repellent Items. Also Destroy 5 Eggs Throughout 1F And 2F. "Ghost Can Kill You."
  • 2F Around The Corner Of Gym Storage Room. Encounter Ghost, Woman Who Hung Herself. Scripted Ghost. "Ghost Is Passive."
  • Main Building Area 1 And Main Building Area 2. Encounter Ghost, Haunted Head Ghost. She Will Find You. "Ghost Can Inflict Damage."

~ Main Building, Section 2 ~

  • 1F - Faculty Office 1. Find Misc. Item, #1 Pin. On Table.
  • 1F - Faculty Office 1. Find Document, Faculty Note. Included Due To The Puzzle Being Tricky. Make Sure You Grab It For Reference Later.
  • 1F - Girls Bathroom. Find Misc. Item, #2 Paper Origami Crane. In Trash Can In 4th Toilet.
  • 1F - Night Duty Room. Encounter Ghost, Lying Female Student. On The Floor. Make Sure To Touch Her And See If She Is OK, Respectfully Of Course. "Ghost Is Passive."
  • 1F - Nurses Office. Find Misc. Item, #3 Paper Origami Crane. On The Bed Behind Partition.
  • 1F - Principals Office. Find Misc. Item, Wooden Handle. In Desk Drawer.
  • 1F - Principals Office. Find Misc. Item, Videotape. In Desk Drawer.
  • 2F - Student Department Office. Find Misc. Item, Eye-Testing Lens (Left). On A Chair.
  • 2F - Student Department Office. Reference Faculty Note. As Well As Explanation Here On The Wiki For Chalkboard Puzzle. You Can Get Stuck On This Puzzle Until You Understand The Gimmick.
  • 2F - Faculty Office 2. Find Misc. Item, #2 Pin. On Chalkboard By Facility Lounge.
  • 2F - Faculty Lounge. Find Misc. Item, Typewriter E Key. Near Air-Conditioner.
  • 3F - Corridor. Find Ghost Story, #13 One-Sided Love. In Floor Of The Hall In Front Of Music Room. Walk Back And Forth To Break.
  • 3F - Corridor. Find Ghost Story, #12 Find My Body For Me. At End Of Corridor By Music Room.
  • 3F - Boys Bathroom. Find Misc. Item, 30g Weight. Outermost Toilet Closest To The Door.
  • 3F - Girls Bathroom. Find Misc. Item, #3 Pin. Large Wash Basin.
  • 3F - Girls Bathroom. Encounter Ghost, Housemistress Spirit. Lights Off, Look In 2nd Mirror From Left. Requires Cigarette Item. "Ghost Inflicts Damage."
  • 3F - Science Supplies Room. Find Misc. Item, 10g Weight. Hidden In Glass Door Shelfs.
  • 4F - Biology Lab. Find Ghost Story, #10 Competitive Spirit. On Floor Under Table.
  • 4F - Biology Lab. Encounter Ghost, Mannequin Ghost. Get Really Dizzy By Crouching And Standing Over And Over Fast. Turn Lamp Sitting On Table On. Avoid Getting Caught By Mannequin. "Ghost Can Kill You." Consider Making A Save Before This Encounter In Case You Need To Try Again.
  • 4F - Biology Supplies Room. Find Misc. Item, Preserved Spider. Left Set Of Glass Cabinets At The End Of Room.
  • Multiple Floors. Encounter Ghost/Boss, Diary Keepers Baby. Scripted Ghost/Boss. Ends In Arts Supplies Room. "Ghost Can Kill You In Final Form."
  • Unlike With Main Building Section 1, It’s Safe To Grab Earth Token After Getting Past Timed Event Boss Encounter.

~ Passage Way From Main Building, Section 2 To New Building ~

  • Encounter Ghost, Fox Spirit When You Reach Double Doors. Turn Around And Walk Back Through The Passage. Listen To The Singing Until It Ends. You May Also Need To Look At Claw Marks On Wall To Get Credit. If The Singing Isn't Triggering For You. You May Need To Wait Until You Leave The New Building To Backtrack. You Will Need The Spirit Bell Item To Do So. "Ghost Can Inflict Damage."

~ New Building ~

  • Starting Hall - Western Staircase - Top Stairs. Find Ghost Story, #19 Mermaid. Right Corner Of Green Wall Area.
  • Starting Hall - Western Staircase - Bottom Stairs. Find Misc. Item, Typewriter O Key. Under Stairs In Back Corner Area.
  • 1F - School Store. Encounter Ghost, Starved Ghost. Offer 2 Lunchbox Items On Lunch Tray That Is Sitting On The Counter. "Ghost Inflicts Damage."
  • 1F - Dance Studio. Find Misc. Item, 50g Weight. In Corner Of Dance Studio. Beyond The Mirror.
  • 1F - Dance Studio. Encounter Ghost/Boss, Doppleganger Ghost. Scripted Ghost/Boss. Use CD In Stereo. Mirror The Speakers Placement On Your Side. "Ghost Can Kill You."
  • 1F - Chairman's Office. Puzzle Help, Portraits Order. Screenshot The Portraits Order For Use On Puzzle You Will Backtrack To.
  • 1F - Electrical Room. Find Ghost Story, #18 Mystery In The Biology Lab. On The Floor Between Machine Parts.
  • 1F - Occult Club. Find Misc. Item, Spirit Bell. Need To Solve Clocks Puzzle. Spirit Bell Allows You To Go Back To Previous Building.
  • 1F - Occult Club. Find Misc. Item, L-Rods. Need To Solve Clocks Puzzle. L-Rods For Use At Rooftop Pool Area.
  • 1F - Music Appreciation Room. Find Misc. Item, Eye-Testing Lens (Right). Behind The Stereo Speaker, You May Need To Crouch To Reach It.
  • 1F - Music Appreciation Room. Encounter Ghost/Boss, Girl With A Hidden Face. Scripted Ghost/Boss. Need Yin-Yang Token To Kill Her. "Ghost Can Kill You."
  • 2F - Classroom 3-3. Encounter Ghost, Lying Female Student. On The Floor. Make Sure To Touch Her And See If She Is OK, More Respectfully This Time Of Course. "Ghost Is Passive."
  • 2F - Classroom 3-4. Find Misc. Item, #4 Piece Of Class Schedule (Mathematics). On The Floor.
  • 2F - Classroom 3-4. Find Misc. Item, #3 Paper Origami Crane. On The Whiteboard Shelf.
  • 2F - Classroom 3-4. Take Note Of The Seating Arrangment In The Room For Paper Origami Crane Puzzle.
  • 2F - Earth Science Lab. Find Ghost Story, #15 Kid In The Corner. Need All Weight Items And Preserved Spider Item To Solve Scale Puzzle.
  • 2F - Art Studio. Statue Heads. Stand In Front Of Them Facing The Whiteboard. Lights On Quick Turn Your Character Back And Forth. Enjoy The Show.
  • 3F - Classroom 3-9. Encounter Ghost, Lying Female Student. On The Floor. Make Sure To Touch Her And See If She Is OK, Guess She Doesn't Like Being Touched! "Ghost Inflicts Damage On 3rd Appearance."
  • 3F - Classroom 3-11. Find Misc. Item, #5 Piece Of Class Schedule (Mathematics). Last Piece To Solve Puzzle.
  • 3F - Main Hall. Encounter Ghost, Grandmother Ghost. Random Chance On Bridge. Walk Back And Forth On The 3F Bridge. "Ghost Inflicts Damage."
  • 4F - Reading Room. Find Misc. Item, #4 Pin. Stuck Into Book On Bookshelves.
  • 4F - Reading Room. Find Misc. Item, Small Key Yellow. Inside Drawer.
  • 4F - Reading Room. Encounter Ghost, Library Ghost. Requires Report Card (2nd In Class). Read Open Book On Table. "Ghost Inflicts Damage."
  • 4F - Resource Center. Find Misc. Item, #5 Pin. On The Desk With Computer Near The Window.
  • 4F - Resource Center. Find Ghost Story, #16 Tragedy Brought By Jealousy. Using All 5 Pins On Globe Reference "A Girls Dream" Document From Classroom 3-8.
  • 5F - Main Hall. Find Ghost Story, #14 Mystery In The Dance Studio. Very Top Of Main Hall By Southern Inaccessible Rooftop.
  • Multiple Rooms. Encounter Ghost, Spider Ghost. Scripted Ghost. Use "New Buildings Notice" Document Found In The Faculty Lounge Will Help You Deal With Her. "Ghost Can Kill You."
  • Rooftop. Encounter Ghost/Boss, Mermaid Ghost. Scripted Ghost. Use L-Rods To Help You Cross The Pool Safetly. "Ghost Can Kill You."
  • Once Across Rooftop Swimming Pool, Don't Drain The Water. The Janitor Should Have Despawned Making Collecting Items Easier. Note: Doing This Means You Can't Easily Go Back Across The Pool Until Drained. Don't Forget To Drain The Pool When You Are Done In The Area.
  • Rooftop. Find Misc. Item, Small Key Blue. Swimming Pool Ledge. Main Hall Side.

~ Main Building, Section 1 Backtrack ~

  • Go Back To Passage Way From New Building That Leads Back To Main Building, Section 1.
  • Automatically Using The Spirit Bell Item You Can Now Go Back There.
  • 1F - Starting Area/Foyer. Examine The Furniture Piece For Cleaning The Chalkboard Erasers "It Is Where You Saw Seol Ji-hyeon Leaning Down And Looking At One Of The Tree Ghosts Plant / Eggs Locations Earlier In The Game". Look Underneath The Furniture Piece To Open A Secret Compartment Using The Wooden Handle Item. Obtaining Ghost Story #3 Boy, Kills Girl.
  • 1F - Workshop. Combine Yellow And Red Key To Open The "Box For Counseling" At Classroom 2-2 To Get Morse Code Chart.
  • Dissemble This Key Then Combine The Red And Blue Key To Unlock "Box For Counseling" In Front Of Classroom 2-5 To Get Small Key Dark.
  • Dissemble This Key Then Combine The Dark And Yellow Key To Unlock The "Box For Opinions" In Front Of The Workshop To Get Ghost Story #6 Wailing From The Art Room.
  • Finally Dissemble This Key And Then Combine Dark And Blue Key To Open The "Box For Opinions" In Front Of Gym Storage Room To Find Final Paper Origami Crane.
  • 2F - Classroom 2-7. Place All 5 "Piece Of Class Schedule (Mathematics)" Onto The Schedule To Obtain Ghost Story #11 Incorrectly Solved Math Problem.

~ Main Building, Section 2 Backtrack ~

  • Last Backtrack Area Before Returning To New Building Again.
  • 1F - Nurses Office. You Can Now Use The Two Lenses To Find The Code For The Safe In The Chairman's Office Of The New Building. Look For Circled Numbers And Symbols And Go Line By Line. You Are Looking For 4 Digit Code, Recommend To Take Screenshots.
  • 1F - Faculty Office 1. Rearrange Portraits In This Room Based On Your Screenshot In Chairman's Office To Obtain Ghost Story #20 Missing Children. Answer Phone Again For Easter Egg.
  • 2F - Faculty Office 2. Place Typewriter Keys Onto Typewriter.
  • Use "Dying Message" Document As A Clue To Open The Locked Box In The Student Department Office On 2F.
  • Use Small Master Key To Open The Typewriter Desk To Obtain Ghost Story #1 Housemistress Spirit.
  • 3F - Music Supplies Room. Use Morse Code Chart On The Metronome Here To Get The Code For Music Appreciation Room's Locked Box.
  • A Quick Pendulum Movement Represents A Dot While A Slow Movement Represents A Dash. Everytime The Pendulum Reaches Either End It Makes A Click Noise. When You Start The Pendulum Movement Wait Until The Pendulum Goes To The Left First And Clicks Then Start Reading It From There.

~ New Building, Final Tasks ~

  • 1F - Chairman's Office. Unlock Safe With Code From 1F - Nurses Office To Obtain Ghost Story #2 Uncrossable Bridge.
  • 1F - Music Appreciation Room. Unlock The Locked Box With The Passcode Obtained From The Metronome Puzzle To Obtain Ghost Story #9 Lost Face.
  • 1F - History Room. Solve "Paper Origami Crane" Puzzle On The Frame In History Room Using The 5 Paper Origami Cranes You Have Found To Obtain Ghost Story #8 Extreme Dieting Death.
  • CONGRATULATIONS!!! If You Haven't Received Any Achievement Notification, Please Go Back Through The Checklist To See What You May Have Missed. There Are A Few Areas Of The Game Left Which Are All Self Contained Areas. Good Luck With The Rest Of White Day. I Hope This Checklist Has Been Useful.

Is This A Guide?

~ Not exactly. This Checklist will help you obtain every Miscellaneous Item, Ghost / Boss and Ghost Stories. As well as a few other things, such as the Doodles to unlock a secret character. Other Items that are not listed you will obatin natrually as you progress. Like Healing Items and the Coins to buy them. This Checklists leaves those things as well as some puzzle solutions for you to discover. So search rooms as you would without this Checklist.

How To Use This Checklist

~ The Checklist is based on you playing on "Hard Mode". As you can't get 100% of everything on "Normal" difficulty or below. Should be said that this information pertains to the Remake on PC of White Day and not the Original game. There is also 8 Endings in this game. You get based on how you interact with characters. This Checklist does not lead you to a certain ending. If this is your first time playing White Day. You may want to consider playing on "Normal" first. To give you a feel for the game, as some may find it very difficult. Then with some familiarity and desire to 100% the game. Go back through on "Hard" mode and use this Checklist to help. You can still use this Checklist for "Normal", just not everything will apply. White Day is not a super long game once you know what to do. A good way to use this Checklist is when you are ready to leave a certain area. Reference it then so you don't ruin the game for yourself. Remember, Puzzle solutions and the way forward to progress is explained to you in the multitude of Documents you get in the game.

~ The sorting method for the Checklist may need some adjustments to be the most effective and efficient. So please, as stated above, drop me an email if it can be improved. It isn't designed for a speedrun or anything. It is setup based on ( Buildings > Floors > Rooms ) you have access to. There is a certain degree of backtracking needed in White Day.

~ The six buttons you see on the ( Playthrough ) page. Clicking them so the Eye Symbol is crossed out will hide that category in the Checklist. Helpful to simplify the Checklist if you only need one category visible.

~ The ( Other ) category pertains to Directions or Explanations.

~ There are a total of ( 20 Ghosts ) to encounter and ( 20 Stories ) explaning their story. For this Checklists purposes, I have seperated ( Ghosts ) to 14 and put the other 6 of them into the ( Bosses ) category. My reasoning is for those 6, they are more than just a jumpscare. There is a little more to them, so I qualifed them as ( Bosses ). Be aware that ( Bosses ) can kill you!

If you add ( Ghosts ) and ( Bosses ) categories together you will get a total of 22. This is because the Lying Female Student is listed 3 times, because you encounter her 3 seperate times. But she only counts as 1 ( Ghost ).

Can I Use This For Multiple Characters?
