~ Main Building, Section 1 ~

  • 1st Floor - Starting Area/Foyer. Find Ghost Story, #7 The Secret Of The Pond. Hidden Behind The Fountain.
  • 1st Floor - Starting Area/Foyer. Find Misc. Item, #1 Paper Origami Crane. In Grandfather Clock Drawer.
  • 1st Floor - Boys Bathroom. Find Misc. Item, Cigarettes. On Top Of Last Toilet.
  • 1st Floor - Workshop. Find Doodle, #2 Pigeon. On Wall.
  • 1st Floor - Classroom 2-6. Find Doodle, #4 Ji-Hyeon Fly. On Wall.
  • 1st Floor - Home Economics Classroom. Find Doodle, #3 Cat. On Wall.
  • 1st Floor - Students Records Office. Find Ghost Story, #5 Ghost Tree. In Top Portion Of Copy Machine.
  • 1st Floor - Students Records Office. Find Misc. Item, 5g Weight. Under A Desk.
  • 1st Floor - Main Office. Find Misc. Item, Small Red Key. In lockbox. Need Toolbelt To Open.
  • 1st Floor - North Stairs. Find Misc. Item, Love Letter. At Very Top Of Stairs.
  • 1st Floor - South Stairs. Find Misc. Item, Typewriter N Key. On Stairs Leading To Basement.
  • 1st Floor - South Stairs. Find Doodle, #5 Crying Angel. On Corner Column Of Stairs.
  • 1st Floor - Classroom 2-1. Find Misc. Item, #1 Piece Of Class Schedule (Mathematics). On Class Schedule On Wall.
  • 1st Floor - Classroom 2-2. Find Doodle, #1 Stick Insect. On Wall.
  • 1st Floor - Classroom 2-4. Find Misc. Item, 1g Weight. In A Cabinet.
  • 1st Floor - Classroom 2-5. Find Misc. Item, #2 Piece Of Class Schedule (Mathematics). On Class Schedule On Wall.
  • 1st Floor - Classroom 2-5. Find Misc. Item, Report Card (2nd In Class). In A Locker.
  • 2nd Floor - Broadcasting Room. Find Ghost Story, #4 Woman Locked In The Closet. On A Table.
  • 2nd Floor - Recording Room. Find Misc. Item, Bloody Workbook. On The Floor.
  • 2nd Floor - Recording Room. Find Doodle, #10 Butterfly. On Wall.
  • 2nd Floor - Window Near Gym Storage Room. Encounter Ghost, Girl Outside The Window. Look At Bloody Handprints.
  • 2nd Floor - Gym Storage Room. Find Misc. Item, Gym Teacher Cap. Inside Big Cabinet With Medals.
  • 2nd Floor - Gym Storage Room. Find Misc. Item, Typewriter M Key. Opposite Side Of The Big Cabinet.
  • 2nd Floor - Long Hallway Past Gym Storage. Find Doodle, #6 Rat With Crutches. On Wall.
  • 2nd Floor - Back Out Of Long Hallway Area. Find Doodle, #7 Birdcage. On Wall Of Gym Storage Room.
  • 2nd Floor - Classroom 2-7. Encounter Ghost, Kid Solving A Problem. Requires Chalk And Bloody Workbook Items.
  • 2nd Floor - Classroom 2-7. Find Doodle, #9 Seong - Cat. On Wall. Is Not Clickable 1st Playthrough.
  • 2nd Floor - Classroom 2-8. Find Misc. Item, Chalk. Laying On Chalkboard Tray.
  • 2nd Floor - Classroom 2-8. Find Ghost Story, #17 Voice That Beckons Me. In A Locker.
  • 2nd Floor - Classroom 2-9. Find Doodle, #8 Lollipop And SYJM. On Wall.
  • 2nd Floor - Classroom 2-11. Encounter Ghost, Attendance Ghost. Requires Gym Teacher's Cap. Look At Book On Lecture Stand. Ghost Can Deal Some Damage.
  • 2nd Floor - Classroom 2-12. Find Misc. Item, #3 Piece Of Class Schedule (Mathematics). On Class Schedule On Wall.
  • Any Classroom Locker. Encounter Ghost, Woman Locked In The Closet. Room Lights Off And Lighter Out. Can Spam Open/Close The Same Locker To Trigger.
  • All Bathrooms Except 1st Floor Girls Bathroom. Encounter Ghost, Face Filled With Hatred. Lay Down In Furtherest Toilet From Door And Look Between Gap And Listen For Audio To Trigger.
  • 1st And 2nd Floor. Encounter Ghost/Boss, Tree Ghost. Scripted Fight. Will Need Chemical Solvent And Insect Repellent Items. Also Destroy 5 Eggs Throughout 1st And 2nd Floor.
  • 2nd Floor Around The Corner Of Gym Storage. Encounter Ghost, Woman Who Hung Herself. Scripted Ghost.
  • Main Building Area 1 And Main Building Area 2. Encounter Ghost, Haunted Head Ghost. She Will Find You.
      ~~~ This Is Best Done Before Grabbing The Wood Token After Defeating Tree Ghost. Will Keep Janitor From Respawning And Leave You In Peace. ~~~

~ Main Building, Section 2 ~

  • 1st Floor - Faculty Office 1. Find Misc. Item, #1 Pin. On Table.
  • 1st Floor - Girls Bathroom. Find Misc. Item, #2 Paper Origami Crane. In Trash Can In 4th Toilet.
  • 1st Floor - Night Duty Room. Encounter Ghost, Lying Female Student. On The Floor. Make Sure To Touch Her, Respectfully Of Course.
  • 1st Floor - Nurses Office. Find Misc. Item, #3 Paper Origami Crane. On The Bed Behind Partition.
  • 1st Floor - Principals Office. Find Misc. Item, Wooden Handle. In Desk Drawer.
  • 1st Floor - Principals Office. Find Misc. Item, Videotape. In Desk Drawer.
  • 2nd Floor - Student Department Office. Find Misc. Item, Eye-Testing Lens (Left). On A Chair.
  • 2nd Floor - Faculty Office 2. Find Misc. Item, #2 Pin. On Chalkboard By Facility Lounge.
  • 2nd Floor - Faculty Lounge. Find Misc. Item, Typewriter E Key. Near Air-Conditioner.
  • 3rd Floor - Corridor. Find Ghost Story, #13 A One-Sided Love. In Floor In Front Of Music Room. Walk Back And Forth To Break.
  • 3rd Floor - Corridor. Find Ghost Story, #12 Find My Body For Me. At End Of Corridor By Music Room.
  • 3rd Floor - Boys Bathroom. Find Misc. Item, 30g Weight. Outermost Toilet Closest To The Door.
  • 3rd Floor - Girls Bathroom. Find Misc. Item, #3 Pin. Large Wash Basin.
  • 3rd Floor - Girls Bathroom. Encounter Ghost, Housemistress Spirit. Lights Off, Look In 2nd Mirror From Left. Requires Cigarette Item. Ghost Can Inflicted Damage.
  • 3rd Floor - Science Supplies Room. Find Misc. Item, 10g Weight. Hidden In Glass Door Shelfs.
  • 4th Floor - Biology Lab. Find Ghost Story, #10 Competitive Spirit. On Floor Under Table.
  • 4th Floor - Biology Lab. Encounter Ghost, Mannequin Ghost. Get Really Dizzy By Crouching And Standing Over And Over Fast. Turn Lamp Sitting On Table On. Avoid Getting Caught By Mannequin. Consider Making A Save Before This Encounter In Case You Need To Try Again.
  • 4th Floor - Biology Supplies Room. Find Misc. Item, Preserved Spider. Left Set Of Glass Cabinets At The End Of Room.
  • Multiple Floors. Encounter Ghost/Boss, Diary Keepers Baby. Scripted Ghost/Boss. Ends In Arts Supplies Room.

~ Passage Way From Main Building, Section 2 To New Building ~

  • Encounter Ghost, Fox Spirit When You Reach Double Doors. Turn Around And Walk Back Through The Passage. Listen To The Singing Until It Ends. You May Also Need To Look At Claw Marks On Wall To Get Credit.

~ New Building ~

  • Western Staircase - Top Stairs. Find Ghost Story, #19 Mermaid. Right Corner Of Green Wall Area.
  • Western Staircase - Bottom Stairs. Find Misc. Item, Typewriter O Key. Under Stairs In Back Corner Area.
  • Main Hall. Find Ghost Story, #14 Mystery Of The Dance Studio. Very Top Of Main Hall By Southern Inaccessible Rooftop.
  • Main Hall. Encounter Ghost, Grandmother Ghost. Random Chance On Bridge. Walk Back And Forth On The Third Floor Bridge.
  • 1st Floor - School Store. Encounter Ghost, Starved Ghost. Offer 2 Lunchbox Items On Lunch Tray. Ghost Can Deal Some Damage.
  • 1st Floor - Dance Studio. Find Misc. Item, 50g Weight. In Corner Of Dance Studio. Beyond The Mirror.
  • 1st Floor - Dance Studio. Encounter Ghost/Boss, Doppleganger Ghost. Scripted Ghost/Boss. Use CD In Stereo. Mirror The Speakers Placement. Heavy Damage Inflicted If You Take Too Long. High Chance Of Death On First Attempt.
  • 1st Floor - Chairman's Office. Puzzle Help, Portraits Order. Screenshot The Portraits Order For Use On Puzzle You Will Backtrack To.
  • 1st Floor - Electrical Room. Find Ghost Story, #18 Mystery In The Biology Lab. On The Floor Between Machine Parts.
  • 1st Floor - Occult Club. Find Misc. Item, Spirit Bell. Need To Solve Clocks Puzzle. Spirit Bell Allows You To Go Back To Previous Building.
  • 1st Floor - Occult Club. Find Misc. Item, L-Rods. Need To Solve Clocks Puzzle. L-Rods For Use At Roof Pool Area.
  • 1st Floor - Music Appreciation Room. Find Misc. Item, Eye-Testing Lens (Right). Behind The Stereo Speak. May Need To Crouch To Reach.
  • 1st Floor - Music Appreciation Room. Encounter Ghost/Boss, Girl With A Hidden Face. Scripted Ghost/Boss. Need Yin-Yang Token To Kill Her.
  • 2nd Floor - Classroom 3-3. Encounter Ghost, Lying Female Student. On The Floor. Make Sure To Touch Her, Respectfully Of Course.
  • 2nd Floor - Classroom 3-4. Find Misc. Item, #4 Piece Of Class Schedule (Mathematics). On The Floor.
  • 2nd Floor - Classroom 3.4. Find Misc. Item, #3 Paper Origami Crane. On The Whiteboard Shelf.
  • 2nd Floor - Earth Science Lab. Find Ghost Story, #15 Kid In The Corner. Need All Weight Items And Preserved Spider. Solve Scale Puzzle.
  • 2nd Floor - Art Studio. Statue Heads. Stand In Front Of Them Facing The Whiteboard. Lights On Quick Turn Your Character Back And Forth. Enjoy The Show.
  • 3rd Floor - Classroom 3-9. Encounter Ghost, Lying Female Student. On The Floor. Make Sure To Touch Her, Respectfully Of Course.
  • 3rd Floor - Classroom 3-11. Find Misc. Item, #5 Piece Of Class Schedule (Mathematics). Last Piece To Solve Puzzle.
  • 4th Floor - Reading Room. Find Misc. Item, #4 Pin. Stuck Into Book On Bookshelves.
  • 4th Floor - Reading Room. Find Misc. Item, Small Key Yellow. Inside Drawer.
  • 4th Floor - Reading Room. Encounter Ghost, Library Ghost. Requires Report Card (2nd In Class). Read Open Book On Table. Ghost Can Damage You.
  • 4th Floor - Resource Center. Find Misc. Item, #5 Pin. On The Desk With Computer Near The Window.
  • 4th Floor - Resource Center. Find Ghost Story, #16 Tragedy Brought By Jealousy. Using All 5 Pins On Globe Reference "A Girls Dream" Document From Classroom 3-8.
  • Multiple Rooms. Encounter Ghost, Spider Ghost. Scripted Ghost. Use "New Buildings Notice" Document Found In The Faculty Lounge Will Help You Deal With Her.
  • Rooftop. Encounter Ghost/Boss, Mermaid Ghost. Scripted Ghost. Use L-Rods To Help You Cross The Pool Safetly.
  • Rooftop. Find Misc. Item, Small Key Blue. Swimming Pool Ledge. Main Hall Side.
      ~~~ Cross The Rooftop Swimming Pool But Don't Drain The Water. The Janitor Will Have Despawned Making Collecting Items Easier. ~~~

~ Main Building, Section 1 Backtrack ~

  • Go Back To Passage Way From New Building That Leads Back To Main Building, Section 1.
  • Automatically Using The Spirit Bell Item You Can Now Go Back There.
  • 1st Floor - Workshop.
  • Combine Yellow And Red Key To Open The Box For Counseling At Classroom 2-2 To Get Morse Code Chart.
  • Dissemble This Key Then Combine The Red And Blue Key To Unlock Box For Counseling In Front Of Classroom 2-5 To Get Small Key Dark.
  • Dissemble This Key Then Combine The Dark And Yellow Key To Unlock The Box For Opinion In Front Of The Workshop To Get Ghost Story #6 Wailing From The Art Room.
  • Finally Dissemble This Key And Then Combine Dark And Blue Key To Open The Box For Opinion In Front Of The Gym Storage Room To Find Final Paper Origami Crane.
  • 1st Floor - Corridor.
  • Examine The Duster Cleaning Furniture Piece In The Starting Area/Foyer And Look Underneath To Open A Secret Compartment Using The Wooden Handle Item. Obtaining Ghost Story #3 Boy Kills Girl.
  • 2nd Floor - Classroom 2-7.
  • Place All 5 "Piece Of Class Schedule (Mathematics)" Onto The Schedule To Obtain Ghost Story #11 An Incorrectly Solved Math Problem.

~ Main Building, Section 2 Backtrack ~

  • Last Backtrack Area Before Returning To New Building Again.
  • 1st Floor - Nurses Office.
  • You Can Now Use The Two Lenses To Find The Code For The Safe In The Chairman's Office Of The New Building. Look For Circled Numbers And Symbols And Go Line By Line. You Are Looking For 4 Digit Code, Recommend To Take Screenshots.
  • 1st Floor - Faculty Office 1.
  • Rearrange Portraits In Faculty Room To Obtain Ghost Story #20 Missing Children. Answer Phone Again For Easter Egg.
  • 2nd Floor - Faculty Office 2.
  • Place Typewriter Keys Onto Typewriter.
  • Use "Dying Message" Document As A Clue To Open The Locked Box In The Student Department Office On 2nd Floor.
  • Use Small Master Key To Open The Typewriter Desk To Obtain Ghost Story #1 Housemistress Spirit.
  • The Capital Letters In Unusual Places Spell A Number.
  • 3rd Floor - Music Supplies Room
  • Use Morse Code Chart On The Metronome Here To Get The Code For Music Appreciation Room's Locked Box.
  • A Quick Pendulum Movement Represents A Dot While A Slow Movement Represents A Dash.
  • Everytime The Pendulum Reaches Either End It Makes A Click Noise.
  • When You Start The Pendulum Movement Wait Until The Pendulum Goes To The Left First And Clicks Then Start Reading It From There.

~ New Building, Final Tasks ~

  • 1st Floor - Chairman's Office.
  • Unlock Safe With Code From 1st Floor - Nurses Office To Obtain Ghost Story #2 Uncrossable Bridge.
  • 1st Floor - Music Appreciation Room.
  • Unlock The Locked Box With The Passcode Obtained From The Metronome To Obtain Ghost Story #9 Lost Face.
  • 1st Floor - History Room.
  • Solve "Paper Origami Crane" Puzzle On The Frame In History Room Using The 5 Paper Origami Cranes You Have Found To Obtain #8 Extreme Dieting Death
  • Take Note Of The Seating Arrangment In Classroom 3-4 On 2nd Floor For Paper Origami Puzzle.
  • Congratulations You Have Finally Completed The Truly Daunting Task Of Completing The Entirety Of The Ghost Collection.
  • If You Haven't Received Any Achievement Notifying You Have Completed It Then Be Sure To Go Back Through The Checklists In This Guide To See What You May Have Missed.
  • Good Luck With The Rest Of The Game, You're Almost Finished. I Hope This Checklist Has Been Useful.

Is This A Guide?

Not exactly. This is a set of checklists and information that you can use while playing White Day. This is useful to to make sure you don't miss an important key item or boss. I think this is cool to use in a second playthrough, once you don't mind so much about spoilers and want to do everything

Can I Use This For Multiple Characters?

Yes, use the profile selector and buttons in the options tab at the top of the page to setup multiple profiles.

How Does The Checklist Status Get Saved?

The checklist is saved to your browser's local storage. Be careful when clearing your browser's cache as it will also destroy your saved progress.
